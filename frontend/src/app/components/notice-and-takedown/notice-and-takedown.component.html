DMCA Takedown Policy

Welcome to Musica's Guide to the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you've received a DMCA takedown notice targeting content you've posted on Musica or if you're a rights-holder looking to issue such a notice, this page will hopefully help to. This guide isn't legal advice and shouldn't be taken as such.

What is the DMCA

The DMCA is a copyright liability safety net for internet service providers hosting allegedly infringing user-generated content. Essentially, so long as a service provider follows the DMCA's notice-and-takedown rules, it won't be liable for copyright infringement based on user-generated content.

Takedown Procedure

In your complaint, please include:

  2. Identify copyrighted work
  3. Idenfity material that is being copyrighted by the work above
  4. Explain what needs to be done to remedy the copyright
  5. Provide your contact information
  6. Provide contact information of the alleged person commiting copyright, if possible
